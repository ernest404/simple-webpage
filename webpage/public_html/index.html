Biography

Hello, I'm Mercyline, and here's a brief biography about me. I was born in Nandi County on the 20th of May, 1997. For my primary education, I attended FR. Kuhn Girls' Primary School - Nandi County. There, I completed my Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) in the year 2010, achieving a mean grade of 'B'. I then progressed to secondary education at St. Joseph's Girls' High School - Chepterit, where I graduated in 2014 with a mean grade of 'B'. Following this, I pursued higher education at Maseno University, where I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Business Studies with Information Technology in 2019. Most recently, in 2022, I completed a Higher Diploma in Business Administration at Kenya Technical Trainers College. Since then, I have been engaged in the teaching profession. I worked at Equip Africa Institute in the Business Department and also collaborated with Go Care Institute, where I remained until the 31st of August.
